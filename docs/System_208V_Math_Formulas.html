1. Shared math helpers

Constants: $\sqrt{3} = 1.7320508075688772$, near-zero $\varepsilon = 10^{-9}$.

Three-phase average

$$ \operatorname{avg}(x_A, x_B, x_C) = \frac{x_A + x_B + x_C}{3} $$

If any phase is missing → blank. Also used for Auto total THD percentages (reporting convention only).

Unit scale (Auto power / reactive)

$$ P_{\mathrm{kW}} = \frac{P_{\mathrm{W}}}{1000},\quad S_{\mathrm{kVA}} = \frac{S_{\mathrm{VA}}}{1000},\quad Q_{\mathrm{kvar}} = \frac{Q_{\mathrm{var}}}{1000} $$

Reactive power (Auto)

Primary: instrument Q-* (signed), converted var → kvar.

$$ Q_{\mathrm{kvar}} = \frac{Q_{\mathrm{var}}}{1000} $$

Fallback only if Q-* is blank/NAN and the triangle is valid:

$$ Q_{\mathrm{fallback}} = \sqrt{S^2 - P^2} \quad\text{when } S^2 \ge P^2 $$
Invalid triangle: if $|P| > |S|$ beyond a tiny rounding tolerance, fallback is N/A — not $\sqrt{|S^2-P^2|}$.

Fallback total PF only

$$ \mathrm{PF}_{\mathrm{total}} = \begin{cases} \mathrm{PF}_{\mathrm{SIGMB/SIGMA}} & \text{if present}\\[0.35em] P/S & \text{if PF blank and } |S| > \varepsilon \end{cases} $$

Phase PFA/PFB/PFC use instrument fields only (no $P/S$ fallback).

Neutral proxy & unbalance (Auto)

$$ I_N = \max(I_A,I_B,I_C) - \min(I_A,I_B,I_C) $$
$$ U_{\mathrm{unbl}}(\%) = \frac{\max_i |x_i - \bar{x}|}{|\bar{x}|}\times 100,\quad \bar{x}=\operatorname{avg}(x_A,x_B,x_C) $$

Signed angle wrap

$$ \operatorname{wrap}(\theta)\in(-180^\circ,180^\circ] $$

Circular Δdeg

$$ \Delta = \operatorname{wrap}\!\big( \operatorname{wrap}(\theta_{\mathrm{meter}}) - \operatorname{wrap}(\theta_{\mathrm{auto}}) \big) $$

2. Auto voltage modes

IIR — line-to-neutral (ch 4/5/6)

$$ \begin{aligned} U_A &= U_{\mathrm{ac4}},\quad U_B = U_{\mathrm{ac5}},\quad U_C = U_{\mathrm{ac6}} \\ U_{\mathrm{LN}} &= \operatorname{avg}(U_{\mathrm{ac4}},U_{\mathrm{ac5}},U_{\mathrm{ac6}}) \\ U_{\mathrm{AB}} &= U_{\mathrm{ac4}}\sqrt{3},\quad U_{\mathrm{BC}} = U_{\mathrm{ac5}}\sqrt{3},\quad U_{\mathrm{CA}} = U_{\mathrm{ac6}}\sqrt{3} \\ U_{\mathrm{LL}} &= U_{\mathrm{LN}}\sqrt{3} \end{aligned} $$
Assumption: ideal balanced $U_{LL}=\sqrt{3}\,U_{LN}$ so L–L columns can sit next to Acuvim L–L fields.

IIW — line-to-line (ch 1/2/3)

$$ \begin{aligned} U_A,U_B,U_C,U_{\mathrm{LN}} &= \text{N/A} \\ U_{\mathrm{AB}} &= U_{\mathrm{ac1}},\quad U_{\mathrm{BC}} = U_{\mathrm{ac2}},\quad U_{\mathrm{CA}} = U_{\mathrm{ac3}} \\ U_{\mathrm{LL}} &= \operatorname{avg}(U_{\mathrm{ac1}},U_{\mathrm{ac2}},U_{\mathrm{ac3}}) \end{aligned} $$

Phase displacement (Acuvim → Auto Phi)

$$ \begin{aligned} \phi_{A,\mathrm{meter}} &= \operatorname{wrap}(IA_{UA}-UA) \\ \phi_{B,\mathrm{meter}} &= \operatorname{wrap}(IB_{UA}-UB) \\ \phi_{C,\mathrm{meter}} &= \operatorname{wrap}(IC_{UA}-UC) \\ \phi_{A,\mathrm{auto}} &= \operatorname{wrap}(-\mathrm{Phi}_4) \quad\text{(similarly for B/C / IIW)} \end{aligned} $$

If a phase voltage is missing, the corresponding current displacement is cleared (N/A), not left as a raw UA-relative angle.

3. Error % and phase averages

Error percent

$$ \mathrm{Error\%} = \frac{\bar{x}_{\mathrm{meter}} - \bar{x}_{\mathrm{auto}}}{\bar{x}_{\mathrm{auto}}}\times 100 $$
  • Either side missing → N/A
  • $|\bar{x}_{\mathrm{auto}}|\le 10^{-9}$ → N/A (never invent zero)
Sign of Auto matters. When Auto is positive, positive Error% means the meter is algebraically higher. When Auto is negative (e.g. signed Q): meter $=-9$, Auto $=-10$: $$\mathrm{Error\%}=\frac{-9-(-10)}{-10}\times 100=-10\%$$ Always read the formula, not only “higher / lower” wording.

Band average — arithmetic (Real-Time / THD)

$$ \bar{x}_c = \frac{1}{|U_c|}\sum_{i\in U_c} x_{i,c} $$

Band average — circular (Phase tables)

$$ \bar\theta = \operatorname{atan2}\!\left( \frac{1}{n}\sum_i \sin\theta_i,\; \frac{1}{n}\sum_i \cos\theta_i \right) \quad\text{then wrap to }(-180^\circ,180^\circ] $$

Avoids the linear-mean bug: $179^\circ$ and $-179^\circ$ average near $\pm 180^\circ$, not $0^\circ$.

4. Load banding

Reference current: Auto SIGMB $I$ after transform. For each sample and target $I_k^{\mathrm{tgt}}$:

$$ e_k = \frac{\big|I - I_k^{\mathrm{tgt}}\big|}{I_k^{\mathrm{tgt}}}\times 100 $$

Keep targets with $e_k \le T$ (default $T=5\%$). Assign to the smallest $e_k$. Meter rows match by timestamp within 60 s default.

Auto total THD (reporting convention)

$$ U_{\mathrm{THD,total}} = \operatorname{avg}(U_{\mathrm{THD},A},U_{\mathrm{THD},B},U_{\mathrm{THD},C}) $$
R&D decision: this is the arithmetic mean of three phase THD percentages, not a physically aggregated THD of a combined waveform unless fundamentals are equal or the instrument defines “total” the same way.
